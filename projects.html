Shadows of Eternia Shadows of Eternia is a multiplayer, 8-bit, adventure, RPG game. It was created in a team of 5, using Typescript, Firebase, React, Tailwind, and Phaser. This project was made in 3 weeks and seemlessly incorporated multiplayer into it. You could adventure with up to 3 different people and venture off to deafet the many slimes, skeletons, goblins, and finally the great skeleton king.
